The PRI Joy RideSM program is a unique experience for those who would like to experience exotic and performance cars without the cost and requirements of a normal car rental. All ages are welcome (though minors require a parental consent form), there are no insurance or damage deposit requirements, and you'll get an unforgettable experience like no other!

Our professional drivers will take up to four passengers for a run through the heart of New York's Hudson Highlands. You'll experience the performance and handling characteristics of two entirely different cars for over an hour as we take you on a cruise through some of the best driving roads in the country.

You'll also receive professional photos and video footage of your Joy RideSM on DVD to share with family and friends for free! We also offer custom album printing and framing for the perfect memory of your ride.
